// Company History

From One Workshop to Southeast Asia

Forge & Form was founded in 2003 by former marine engineer David Tan in a 400 sq ft shophouse in Toa Payoh. With a deep understanding of what tradespeople actually need on the job, David built the company on a simple promise: stock only tools he would trust with his own hands.

By 2008, we had outgrown our first warehouse and relocated to our current 12,000 sq ft facility in Tuas. We began importing directly from factory partners in Germany, Japan, and Taiwan — cutting out intermediaries to deliver better quality at fairer prices.

Today, Forge & Form serves over 8,000 clients across Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, and beyond — from sole-trader electricians to Fortune 500 EPC contractors. We remain family-owned, independently operated, and absolutely committed to the trades.
